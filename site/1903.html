*** Presidential administration's budget for fiscal year 2020

In fiscal year 2019 in the U.S. there was $130M for LBNF & $20(+15?)M for PIP-II. That was out of $980M enacted for HEP. The President's Budget request for 2020 for HEP is $768M, $104M for LBNF. Last year, the House, Senate and Conference committee budgets were all substantially more than the administration's request. The proposed budget implies a 4 year delay. See AIP's FYI #31.

*** Supernova neutrino database

http://asphwww.ph.noda.tus.ac.jp/snn/ This web site provides a series of numerical simulations of supernova neutrino emission from core collapse to neutron star cooling (~20 sec) for various progenitor stellar models (13-50 Msolar with two different metallicities). For details, see Nakazaro er al., http://dx.doi.org/10.1088/0067-0049/205/1/2
