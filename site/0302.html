*** Two Argon Detector Ideas

The LANNDD proposal (Liquid Argon Neutrino and Nucleon Decay Detector) is for a large (~100kton) liquid argon detector to search for nucleon decay and study neutrinos. It is featured in a letter of intent for a long-baseline experiment at Brookhaven. Mini-lanndd is a design for a small version (5 kton) which has been considered (http://arxiv.org/ftp/astro-ph/papers/0206/0206124.pdf) for the NuMI medium energy beam at 730 km for theta_13.

*** Another Liquid Argon Detector

Another idea (from K. McDonald) would place a 40-ton liquid argon module off-axis in the near detector area of the FNAL NuMI beam. The Chicago Cyclotron Magnet coils could give a 1 Tesla field in 2/3 of the detector, which would get 10^5 CC numu events per year.

*** One more Argon Detector

After testing T600 in Pavia in 2001, ICARUS is preparing for installation in LNGS Hall B. An installation program was approved by LNGS in February. Cloning of T600 has been recommended by LNGSSC and CERN-SPSC (for CNGS) to reach T3000. The first T1200 module is funded and the design is ongoing. CNGS (Cern Neutrinos to Gran Sasso) start is now expected in May 2006.

*** KR2DET Reactor proposal to measure theta_13

One detector reactor experiments have an accuracy in mixing angle that is limited by systematic errors of a few percent. If this could be improved to better than 1% with 2 or more detectors, a value of theta_13 could be measured down a factor of 10 from its present limit. A proposal has been made to do this at the Krasnoyarsk Reactor, with two 46 ton detectors at 115 m and 1000 m from the reactor. One advantage at Krasnoyarsk is that there is a 600MWE overburden which reduces backgrounds. Other groups are also looking at other reactors and sites.

*** Neutrino Oscillations and the age of the Earth

The energy dependence of geoneutrinos can be used in KamLAND to measure the Thorium to Uranium ratio. Mohanty uses early data hep-ph/0302060 to determine the age as a function of mixing parameters. In LMA-I it gets the right(?) answer 4.5 Gyears. For the LMA-II solution, the age does not match at 99% CL.

*** 2 more Underground Nu projects on DOE-OS Director Orbach list

Besides 5 of 13 High Energy projects being related to neutrinos, 2 of the 9 nuclear physics projects on the list are next- generation double beta decay (Majorana) and pp solar neutrino detectors. Within the next few weeks, Orbach will take the facility recommendations and prioritize them. He will do this by himself because no committee has been able to successfully prioritize across fields. He described the "superb" support there is for his office's programs, while acknowledging that funding is not sufficient. Draft report in March, final in May.
