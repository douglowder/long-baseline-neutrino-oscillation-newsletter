Long-Baseline news, April 2021

*** Observation of Glashow Resonance by IceCube

A 6.3 PeV nuebar interacting on an electron makes an on-mass- shell W and is known as the Glashow resonance. Examining both contained and partially contained events, IceCube has constructed a boosted decision tree and made a blinded cut at 0.6. One event at 0.65 is consistent with this signal. The cosmic ray background appears at 1.0e-07 events expected.

*** KATRIN updates direct neutrino mass limit

At the APS meeting on 19 April, the KATRIN direct neutrino mass experiment, by measuring the endpoint of Tritium beta decay, updated their limit for each nu mass to less than 800 meV. https://www.sciencenews.org/article/neutrino-max-possible-mass-tiny-new-estimate-particle-physics

*** First publication from MARLEY

http://www.marleygen.org/news_posts/prc40Ar.html A paper on Nuclear de-excitations in low-E nu-e CC scattering on 40Ar has appeared in Phys. Rev. C. MARLEY is the Model of Argon Reactions Low Energy Yields. The paper describes physics models for this process as implemented in MARLEY v1.2.0. https://journals.aps.org/prc/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevC.103.044604
