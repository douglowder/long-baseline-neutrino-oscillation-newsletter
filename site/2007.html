*** Final long-baseline results from MINOS and MINOS+

The MINOS long-baseline program, the original motivation for this newsletter, ran from 2005 to 2016. It accumulated 23.6e20 pot & 61 kt-yr of atmospheric neutrinos. Combined best fit for Normal Order is dmsq_32 = 2.40 + 0.08 - 0.09 e-3 eV^2 and sin^2(theta23) = 0.43 (0.39 to 0.63). It disfavors maximal mixing (.91 sigma), and prefers lower octant (.55 sigma) & normal order (.45 sigma). The data from its search for sterile neutrinos is consistent with 3-flavor paradigm & in a joint fit with Daya Bay excludes large regions of 3+1 parameter space. arXiv:2006.15208 & arXiv:2002.00301

*** MiniBooNE Oscillation Results with Final Dataset

With 6e20 protons on target more than previously published, MiniBooNE, which ran from 2002-2019, has a stable low-energy nue-like excess. The radial distribution disfavors a source from dirt events or pizero background. See arXiv:2006.16883 . Non- oscillation explanations are considered by Brdar et al. in arXiv:2007.14411 . Twenty scenarios are considered there, but data from other experiments rule out most of them.

*** Final datataking on Borexino in 2020

With its discovery of neutrinos from the CNO cycle in the sun and its previous measurement of the pp-cycle neutrinos, Borexino has "completely unraveled the two processes powering the Sun." They have also measured geoneutrinos. There are plans to shut down Borexino, which has been taking data since 2007, and dismantle at the end of 2020. Large Volume Detector (LVD) will also shutdown. https://physicsworld.com/a/gran-sasso-lab-to-shut-down-controversial-experiments/

*** Final datataking from Daya Bay in 2020

The Daya Bay experiment studying neutrinos from nuclear reactors since December 2011 is taking data through the pandemic and will keep running until the end of 2020. They have the most precise measurement of theta13 and expect a final precision of 2.7%. They have observed reactor fuel evolution and disfavor a sterile nu explanation of the reactor antineutrino anomaly. https://indico.fnal.gov/event/43209/contributions/187850/attachments/129087/158762/ling-dayabay-neutrino2020.pdf

*** Final run of ProtoDUNE-SP Phase 1 and first results

The Single Phase Liquid Argon Prototype at CERN for DUNE ended its run on 19 July 2020. It studied liquid argon purity, took test beam events, tested high voltage systems, and successfully tested an injection of Xenon (13.5 kG for 18.8 ppm in mass). Xe shifts 128 nm wavelength to 175 nm which increases the Rayleigh scattering length to increase uniformity & light yield. Results on ProtoDUNE performance can be found in arXiv:2007.06722 .

*** rant - neutrinos are not ghosts

Ghosts have the well known property that they stand on the floor but can go through walls. This was a good metaphor for nus in the 1950's, but I dislike describing our favorite particles as "ghostlike". Furthermore, they are hardly elusive. I would call them ubiquitous. Experiments measure them from many sources with better signal/background than most collider searches.

*** Snowmass moves Neutrino Theory from Neutrino to Theory

"In reviewing the course of the Snowmass 21 process to date (the Theory and Neutrino Frontier conveners) realized that the current state of affairs in relation to the Neutrino Theory topical group was entirely unintentional. We therefore decided to transfer the Neutrino Theory topical group (NF08) with its current conveners to the Theory Frontier as topical group Neutrino Theory (TF11). The liaisons will stay in place, but will now liaise from TF to NF."
