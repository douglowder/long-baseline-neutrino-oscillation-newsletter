Long-Baseline news, March 2024

*** Will we see neutrinos from T Coronae Borealis?

https://www.bbc.com/future/article/20240322-visible-nova-explosion-is-coming A nova @ 1 kpc visible during the day is expected in a few months. Supernovae emit neutrinos, but what about novae? Look anyway but https://astrobites.org/2022/10/13/no-nova-neutrinos/ says no.

*** Fermilab Beam restarts

After a longer-than-expected shutdown, the Fermilab neutrino beam has restarted, and the NuMI power was 812 kW as I write this. https://programplanning.fnal.gov/accelerator-experiment-status/

*** 2024 Neutrino Oscillation Workshop in Italy

https://home.ba.infn.it/~now/now2024/ The biannual Neutrino Oscillation Workshop (NOW 2024) will be held 2-8 September in Otranto (Lecce Italy). The four pillars of oscillations are 1.) standard 3-nu oscillations, 2.) Beyond Standard nu framework, 3.) Neutrino mass states and interactions, & 4.) From the sky & cosmos

*** Neutrino 2024 16-22 June 2024 in Milano

Due to the exceptional number of abstract submissions for Neutrino 2024, far exceeding the space available to display them, the time required to down-select is taking much longer than expected. Submitters have already started to receive their approval or rejection, but more time is needed to complete the process.

*** NuFact 2024

This will be 16-21 September at Argonne in Illinois. Registration will probably open early May. https://indico.fnal.gov/event/63406/

*** Neutrino Beam Class 15-19 July in Rohnert Park California

At the U.S. Particle Accelerator School this year there will be a 1-week course on Neutrino Beams. The course is open to all levels of participants. Substantial support will be provided for for grad students, who can earn graduate-level credit transferable to their university. https://uspas.fnal.gov/programs/2024/rohnertpark/

*** Invisibles School in Bologna 24-28 June 2024

Invisibles School 2024 ( https://agenda.infn.it/event/39074/) will take place in Bologna, Italy. It precedes Invisibles24 Workshop. https://agenda.infn.it/event/39073/ which will take place 1-5 July. They aim at a broad audience working in the areas of neutrino, dark matter, astroparticle physics, cosmology.

*** How long is long-baseline neutrino news?

It is not long, but the baselines of some neutrino experiments are long. I stop at 111 lines of ASCII text, and it can be printed on two 8.5" x 11" sheets of paper. Why are newspaper pages so large? The original rationale was that the British enacted in 1712 a tax on newspapers based on the number of pages in each edition.

*** Long-Baseline Neutrino Facility Project Director retires

https://inside.fnal.gov/2024/04/chris-mossey-lbnf-dune-us-project-director-retires/ (requires FNAL services account to access). Admiral Chris Mossey, who joined Fermilab in August of 2015, is retiring after eight years and 800,000 tons of excavated rock (approximately equal to the mass of eight Nimitz class aircraft carriers, by his figuring). In the picture in the above link, Chris is standing next to my daughter who represented Warrenville.
