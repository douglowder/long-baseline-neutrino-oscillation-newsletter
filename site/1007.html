*** 90% for MINOS; 90% for MINERvA at Fermilab

Is that 180%? MINOS has asked for more antineutrino running, while Minerva prefers the higher rates from Neutrinos. The Fermilab PAC "recommends sharing the shortfall of POT such that MINOS receives ~90% of the total requested POT for antineutrino running and MINERvA receives ~90% of the total requested POT for neutrino running - assuming the current operation capabilities of the NuMI beamline".

*** MiniBooNE to end early?

The PAC and FNAL management decided to allow MicroBooNE use the MiniBooNE detector hall. This requires MiniBooNE to end the run in May 11 instead of the start of the 2012 shutdown, to dismantle it and to give the hall over to mircoBooNE. MiniBooNE would only get to 8E20 POT, which would fall short of doubling the current analyzed data set. They object to this new plan. Their recent antineutrino results with 5.7E20 POT is consistent with LSND at the 2.7 sigma level. However, it desperately needs more data to make it statistically robust. In fact, the fitted significance improves up to 20E20 POT (where stat error no longer dominates).

*** Super-K Solar neutrinos; day/night effect at 90% CL.

New Solar results from Super-K III were presented at Neutrino 2010 http://indico.cern.ch/conferenceTimeTable.py?confId=73981#all.detailed A tight fiducial volume cut is applied for the lowest energy events, E<5.5 MeV. The systematic error on the total flux has been reduced by precise calibrations and software improvements from 3.5% in SK-1 to 2.1% in SK-III. The 8B flux is 2.32 +- 0.04 (stat) +- 0.05(syst) 1e6/cm^2/s. The day night ratio 2(D-N)/(D+N) is -0.056 +- 0.031(stat) +- 0.013(syst.) for SK-III. All Super-K day/night measurements have the same sign, and combined, it is two sigma (stat only) from zero and consistent with the best fit oscillation results.

NuFACT2010 is the twelfth edition of a series of workshops started in 1999, whose main goal is to understand the different options for a future neutrino oscillation experiment able to attack the problems of mass hierarchy and CP violation in the leptonic sector. 20-25 Oct 2010 at Tata Institute for Fundamental Research.
