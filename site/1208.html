Long-Baseline news, August 2012

*** European Strategy for Accelerator-Based Neutrino Physics

The community of European physicists involved in nu oscillation experiments is strong enough to support a major nu long-baseline project in Europe, and has an ambitious, competitive and coherent vision to propose. Two complementary design studies have been carried out: LAGUNA/LBNO, focused on deep underground detector sites, and EUROnu, focused on high intensity neutrino facilities. LAGUNA LBNO recommends, as first step, a conventional neutrino beam CN2PY from a CERN SPS North Area Neutrino Facility (NANF) aimed at the Pyhasalmi mine in Finland. A sterile neutrino search experiment which could also be situated in the CERN north area has been proposed (ICARUS-NESSIE) using a 2 detector set-up, allowing a definitive answer to the 20 year old question open by the LSND experiment. EUROnu concluded that a 10 GeV Neutrino Factory, aimed at a magnetized neutrino detector situated, also, at a baseline of around 2200 km (+-30%), would constitute the ultimate neutrino facility; A 13 page report prepared by the program committee of the May 2012 European Town Meeting is at arXiv:1208.0512.

*** SciBooNE/MiniBooNE dual baseline search

A joint analysis of these two experiments in the Fermilab Booster beam is able to dramatically improve upper limits on numu disappearance in the .1 - 100 eV^2 region. arXiv:1208.0322

*** Mass hierarchy dependence in reactor experiments

Qian et al. arXiv:1208.1551 argues that a 60 km experiment is challenged by finite detector resolution, absolute energy scale calibration, & degeneracies caused by current experimental uncertainty of delta msquared_32. Ciuffoli et al. arXiv:1208.4800 say a result there is spurious because the high energy tail is insensitive to the hierarchy due to exotic isotope decays. Also see arXiv:1208.1991

*** Are Light Sterile Nus preferred or disfavored by cosmology?

Cosmology experiments can measure Neff the # of light particles & the total mass of neutrinos. A sterile nu which shows up in one should show up in the other. The subject above is the title of Joudaki et al., arXiv:1208.4354 Also arXiv:1208.0032 as well as "By Dawn's Early Light..." submitted on 4 July arXiv:1207.1105
