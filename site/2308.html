*** Status of JSNS2/JSNS2-II sterile nu search at J-PARC

http://j-parc.jp/c/en/topics/2023/08/28001197.html JSNS2 searches for neutrino oscillation to sterile nus with a short baseline. JSNS2 had its 3rd run April-June using a single near detector. 4,279×10^21 POT were accumulated for a total of 3.276×10^22 POT since 2020, 29% of the approved POT. New electronics have been tested during this run. For JSNS2-II, a new far detector is under construction. 142 10-inch PMTs out of 228 in total were installed by end of May. The FD acrylic tank was moved into the FD area and is awaiting installation. JSNS2-II will start taking data in FY23.

*** Contract for underground gas liquefication for DUNE

Air Products, an industrial gases company from Pennsylvania has the contract for an underground liquid nitrogen refrigeration system. It will ultimately supply the 17,500 tons of liquid argon for DUNE as part of the Long-Baseline Neutrino Facility.

*** Argon cost for ProtoDUNE in Europe has been rising

The increase in the cost of liquid Argon in Europe will not enable CERN to fill both DUNE prototypes. The proposal is to fill one in early 24, then transfer Ar a few months later to the other. The DUNE EB and FD & Cryogenics management agreed that NP02 (vertical drift) will go first, and then NP04 (horizontal drift). Also see https://www.procurementresource.com/resource-center/argon-price-trends

*** Meld: Feasibility of a Framework-less Framework for analysis

arXiv:2308.16710 discusses that existing frameworks support rigid, collider-based data models which do not map well to nu experiments like DUNE. Meld aims to address this.
