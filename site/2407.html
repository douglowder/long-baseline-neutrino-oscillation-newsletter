*** Neutrino mass; inverted mass ordering ruled out

Earlier I missed arXiv:2404.03002 in which DESI ruled out the inverted order with Sum(m_nu) < 72 meV @95% CL. arXiv:2407.18047 & arXiv:2407.06117 discuss how adding CMB data the limit is Sum < 50 meV and is in conflict with oscillations Sum > 60 meV. arXiv:2407.13831 considers that the limit rules out IO and is at the edge of an NO conflict. Negative masses are discussed in arXiv:2407.07878, while arXiv:2407.02462 , arXiv:2407.15640 & arXiv:2407.10965 show how dark matter and energy mimic it.

*** ProtoDUNE Horizontal Drift cryostat filled and taking data

It was filled in Spring 2024 and has been taking cosmics in May & beam in June. The TPC and cold electronics are new. The HV system has been redesigned, the Photon Detectors (X-Arapucas) and their electronics are new, APAs are an evolution of the original design. HV has ramped up to 180 kV (500 V/m) without problems and is very stable. Ar purity is excellent - e- life above 50 ms, exceeding requirements. The TPC trigger alerted DUNE to an upstream beam halo tracks before the accelerator operators realized conditions had changed. First data with negative beam particles has been taken and calibration systems have been commissioned. NP02, the vertical drift ProtoDUNE, will be filled for beam runs in 2025.

*** Management advice from whistleblowers at Fermilab

A variety of disturbing incidents at Fermilab are related in this document posted to arXiv:2407.13924. The common theme is the management organization, FRA, which is currently out for bid. My observation is that management organizations, be it URA, FRA, or an office of the University of Chicago are fairly irrelevant. In reading some of the stories, I concluded that too much emphasis is put on privacy at the expense of effectiveness and accountability. That thought involves society more than just physics entities. The inappropriate focus on security is mentioned but not central.

*** More Beam power at J-PARC

The neutrino facility achieved 800kW stable operation in June, which exceed original design power of 750kW, and is a big step to realize 1.3 MW in beam power for the Hyper-Kamiokande project. They also cooled and accelerated positive muons for the first time. https://j-parc.jp/c/en/press-release/2024/05/23001341.html
