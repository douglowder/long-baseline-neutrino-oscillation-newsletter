*** Result favors the normal hierarchy

The sum of the neutrino masses with the cosmological analysis in arXiv:1410.7244 is < 140 meV at 95% CL. We would expect ~ 60 meV in the hierarchical normal hierarchy, and 110 inverted. In this paper Palanque-Delabrouille et al. combine the Lyman alpha forest power spectrum from BOSS with Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) Data and Baryon Acoustic Oscillation (BAO) data.

*** MINERvA measures Quasielastic Interactions from the p side

Most Quasi-elastic measurements rely on the final state lepton kinematics to reconstruct an estimate of both the neutrino energy and momentum transferred to the nucleus (Q2). Now for the first time MINERvA has isolated a sample of quasi-elastic-like events where Q2 is reconstructed only by final state proton kinematics & the fact that a muon was also tagged in the interaction but no detected pions. This measurement provides a new probe of the final state interactions. Comparisons with models show that the model that best matches the proton kinematics are not the same as the one that best predicts the muon kinematics. arXiv:1409.4497

*** MINERvA measures Coherent pion production

Previously, K2K & SciBooNE didn't observe coherent pi+ production at the level predicted by Rein-Sehgal as then implemented in NEUT and NUANCE. By selecting events with low momentum transferred to the nucleus, (a model-independent signature of coherent interactions), MINERvA finds 1628 (770) coherent candidates in its nu (antinu) datasets. They find that the predicted coherent pion kinematics from neutrino event generators currently used do not agree with the measured distributions, particularly for pions at large angles with respect to the neutrino beam. arXiv:1409.3835

*** MINERvA measures single pion production

MINERvA has reported the cross section for single pion production on a hydrocarbon target at 4.0 GeV for numu interactions. Events with low W are chosen to enhance single pi production mostly from the Delta (1232). A comparison of the data to models shows the importance of including pion intranuclear rescattering. The cross section as a function of pi angle has a similar shape as MiniBooNE at lower neutrino energy, heightening the disagreement with theory seen previously. arXiv:1406.6415

*** Decoherence of neutrino oscillations 2

Last month I wrote, "As far as I know, only supernova neutrinos are far enough away to decohere from measured sources of nus. Now I know better. pp neutrinos decohere already inside the Sun (at the distance about 200 km from production). Boron neutrinos (due to higher energies) decohere at few solar radii. hep-ph/9903329

*** A coherent answer to a coherency question

To my question on the similarity/difference between Coherent Pion Production (CPP) and Coherent Nu elastic scattering (NES), the biggest difference is that in CPP, coherency is due to the [(virtual meson)+(nucleus)-->(physical pion production)+(nucleus)] interaction, and hence is a strong interaction effect and varies as A**2. In NES, the coherency is due to the [virtual Z + nucleus -->(nucleus]] interaction, and hence is a weak interaction effect varying as [Z(1 - 4*sin^2(theta-W)) - N ]^2, approximating to N^2.

*** RENO-50 funding

The RENO-50 project will receive an R&D funding of US 2M $ from the Samsung Science & Technology Foundation, in next three years. The R&D will include a feasibility study to achieve 3% energy resolution at 1 MeV, essential for determining the neutrino mass hierarchy using reactor neutrinos. During the R&D period, a full funding of RENO-50 will be pursued."

*** CERN SPC welcomes test beam effort and NA61 for LBNF

Thomas Kutter and Greg Pawloski presented EOI011, for a detector- engineering test and test beam calibration of a 1-phase LAr TPC. They supported the project and recognized its potential to foster cooperation & further develop synergies within a future worldwide long-baseline LBNF collaboration. The encouraged a technical proposal that included the synergy with WA105. The also received a proposal for hadron production measurements for FNAL using NA61. They considered the physics case important & recommended approval.
