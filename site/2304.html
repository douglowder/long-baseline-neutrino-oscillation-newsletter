Long-Baseline news, April 2023

"Neutrino Meetings are Back! issue"

*** ISAPP neutrino physics school in Varenna Italy, 27 June-6 July

The International School in AstroParticle Physics 2023 aims to advance the young researchers knowledge about the current status of research in the field of Neutrino Physics and Astrophysics. It will be held in the beautiful small village of Varenna, located in front of the Como Lake and surrounded by the Alps scenario. https://agenda.infn.it/event/32559/

*** WIN2023 in Zhuhai China 3-8 July 2023

The 29th International Workshop on Weak Interactions and Neutrinos (WIN2023) will be at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). The workshop aims to provide a forum for researchers from around the world to exchange the latest developments and progress in the field of weak interactions and neutrinos. An excursion to see JUNO will be offered. https://ifcen.sysu.edu.cn/win2023/

*** ICISE Neutrino Workshop in Quy Nhon Vietnam 16-19 July, 2023

A Neutrino Workshop is being organized at IFIRSE, ICISE center, Vietnam. The workshop intends to bring together experimentalists & theorists to address the unsolved unknowns for the completeness of the 3-flavor paradigm and beyond, to understand anomalies in and tensions among experimental data, and to investigate possible non-standard physics with current and future neutrino experiments. https://ifirse.icise.vn/nugroup/nuworkshop2023/

*** PhD neutrino summer school in Copenhagen Denmark 17-21 July

The Niels Bohr International Academy (NBIA) invites PhD students and advanced Master students to the International Summer School on Neutrinos: Here, There & Everywhere. This one-week school aims to bring the participants up to date with the latest developments in neutrino physics, from theoretical issues to experimental results, including astrophysical and cosmological aspects. https://indico.nbi.ku.dk/event/1961/

*** SLAC Summer Institute in Palo Alto California 7-18 August 2023

The theme for SSI this year will be "Machine Learning Across the Frontiers: Artificial Intelligence in Fundamental Physics". These SSI lectures will introduce methods for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and their successful applications across many areas of fundamental physics. https://indico.slac.stanford.edu/event/7540/overview

*** NuFACT2023 in Seoul Korea 21-26 August 2023

The main goal of the workshop is to review the progress of current and future facilities able to improve on measurements of the properties of neutral and charged lepton flavor violation, as well as searches for new phenomena beyond the capabilities of presently planned experiments. Experimenters, theorists, and accelerator physicists from all over the world will share expertise with the common goal of reviewing the results of currently operating experiments and designing the next generation of experiments. http://nufact2023.snu.ac.kr/

*** AAP Workshop 2023 in York, UK 18-21 September 2023

The upcoming Applied Antineutrino Physics (AAP) Workshop will be held in the historic city of York, United Kingdom, hosted by the UK's Boulby Underground Laboratory. The AAP workshop will provide an excellent opportunity to review and discuss antineutrino detection technology for applications such as reactor monitoring, safeguards and geophysics, and aims to bring together scientific & stakeholder communities. https://indico.ph.liv.ac.uk/event/1195

*** NNN23 in Procida Italy 11-13 October 2023

The 22nd International Workshop on Next Generation Nucleon Decay and Neutrino Detectors (NNN23) will be held in Procida, an island near Naples. The main physics topics of the workshop include searches for proton decay, CP violation in the lepton sector, determination of the neutrino mass ordering, and observation of neutrinos from core-collapse supernovae. https://inspirehep.net/conferences/2652189

*** Neutrino Telescopes in Venice Italy 23-27 October 2023

The Neutrino Telescopes is a workshop that takes place every two years. Its long tradition dates back to the 80's. After the online edition of 2021 due to the pandemic situation, the XX one will be held again in presence in Venice, at "Palazzo Franchetti" of the “Istituto Veneto di Scienze, Lettere ed Arti”. https://agenda.infn.it/event/33107/
