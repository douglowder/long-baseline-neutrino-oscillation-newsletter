Long-Baseline news, March 2014

*** Scientific Opportunities with the Long-Baseline Nu Experiment

The P5 report is due in late May and there's a good chance it will describe the Long-Baseline Neutrino Experiment (LBNE) from FNAL to the Sanford Underground Research Facility as a primary part of the US High Energy Physics Program in the next decade. An updated document which describes the physics programs in some detail is now available. The 286 page document includes descriptions of the beam, the ways to measure CP violation and the mass hierarchy, atmospheric neutrinos, core-collapse supernovae, nucleon decay, and a range of other physics that can be done with a high neutrino flux in a near detector or in an underground far detector. http://lbne2-docdb.fnal.gov/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=8087

*** T2K measures theta_23

The Tokai to Kamiokande (T2K0 long-baseline neutrino experiment has measured the large mixing angle theta_23 using muon nu disappearance. They get sin^2(theta23) = 0.514 +-0.056 for the normal hierarchy and 0.511 +- 0.055 inverted. The best fit mass splitting for the normal hierarchy is 2.51 +- 0.10 10^-3 eV^2 and 2.48 +- 0.10 10^-3 eV^2 inverted from 6.57e20 pot. arXiv:1403.1532

*** B mode polarization and neutrinos

BICEP 2 has measured "B mode polarization" in the ripples of the cosmic microwave background, which if I understand correctly, is expected from an era of inflation in the big bang. At a different frequency, B mode polarization from lensing could provide an error on the sum of the neutrino masses as low as 22 meV. http://www.mpa-garching.mpg.de/~komatsu/meetings/ds2013/schedule/reichardt_20131018.pdf One analysis of BICEP data favors and extra relativistic degree of freedom, 4.00 +- 0.41 http://arxiv.org/pdf/1403.4852v1.pdf

*** Daya Bay spectral measurement and Hydrogen analysis

http://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.112.061801 A high statistics energy measurement of reactor neutrino disappearance has been published by Daya Bay. In 217 days they measured 41589 far and 293721 near antineutrino candidates. They measured sin^2(2q13) = 0.090 + 0.008 - 0.009 and the first direct measurement of delta msquared_ee = 2.59 +.19 -.20 10^-3 eV^2 consistent with 3nu analysis and muon nu disappearance. They have also shown their Hydrogen analysis with sin^2(2q1) = 0.082 +- 0.018 with a combined fit to Gd and shape giving 0.089 + 0.007 - 0.008. https://indico.in2p3.fr/getFile.py/access?contribId=199&sessionId=4&resId=0&materialId=slides&confId=9116
