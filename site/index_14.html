SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS Two subscription methods are available. To receive the (~100 line) newsletter each month, the listname is "long-bnews". You can subscribe by sending a message "subscribe long-bnews firstname lastname" to [email protected] . Put your name, not your email, and put it in the body of the message, not the subject.
To get a one line message that the newsletter is available on the web, the listname is "longbnews_web". You can subscribe by sending a message "subscribe longbnews_web firstname lastname" to [email protected] . If you want to change your e-mail address, send an e-mail message from your old e-mail address to [email protected] with the following message body: "CHANGE listname [email protected]." listname should be long-bnews or longbnews_web as appropriate. Full instructions for additions/deletions/changes can be found at http://listserv.fnal.gov/users.html . To unsubscribe from either list, you could also just contact: Maury Goodman. at [email protected] and send a message in any format that you like.

Links to most neutrino experiments can be found at: The neutrino oscillation industry .
