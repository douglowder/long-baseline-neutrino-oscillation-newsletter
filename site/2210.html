*** Neutrino platform week at CERN in March

In March 2023 there will be a neutrino theory/pheno workshop at CERN. https://indico.cern.ch/e/neutrino-platform-week-2023. The focus will be primarily on nu phenomenology and theory and will cover nus in terrestrial experiments, astrophysics, & cosmology.

*** Sexual Harassment case on neutrino experiments

A shocking case of misconduct has led to an individual's removal from the NOvA and DUNE neutrino experiments and author lists. https://www.theguardian.com/education/2022/oct/12/former-ucl-academic-to-pay-damages-after-harassing-colleague-for-months A Snowmass white paper, "Climate of the Field: Snowmass 2021" in arXiv:2204.03713 discusses the current climate in HEP and astro, and efforts toward providing the community a more diverse, inclusive, and equitable environment. A series of recommendations for the community and the funding agencies includes, "(we) must employ the use of robust strategic planning procedures, including a full re-envisioning of science workplace norms and culture."

*** MicroBooNE limits sterile neutrino oscillations

Previously MicroBooNE didn't see a low-energy excess of electron neutrinos, which seemed to conflict with the sterile neutrino interpretation of the MiniBooNE data. Now in arXiv:2210.10216 they present the first constraints on light sterile neutrino oscillations from combined appearance and disappearance searches. They observe no evidence of light sterile neutrinos, and present exclusion limits. They also point out that cancellation of nue appearance and disappearance effects leads to degeneracy.

*** NOvA Test Beam concludes run

A small scale version of the NOvA Detector in a tertiary FNAL beam of charged particles, to measure + and - pi's, p's, K's & e's in NOvA, is known as NOvA's Test Beam, momentum from 0.5 to 1.25 GeV. The last spill was midnight 9 July. Decommissioning is continuing. Analysis of NOvA's response to these single particles is ongoing.

*** Solar Neutrinos and planet building

We know that the sun is a prolific neutrino source. A few hundred meters from a nuclear reactor, the nu flux from the sun exceeds the antinu flux from the reactor. In arXiv:2210.06900, Kunitomo et al. argue that planet formation processes have a significant contribution to present-day solar nu fluxes.

*** Neutrinos and Jupiter, Neptune, and Asteroid Bennu

In arXiv:2210.04761, French and Sher make a case that Jupiter could be a source of monoenergetic neutrinos from 1-4 GeV WIMP annihilation, whereas in the Sun, those WIMPS would evaporate before annihilation. In arXiv:2210.05913, English and Solomey present a concept study for observing galactic neutrinos in the atmosphere of Neptune. And Tsai et al. use accurate tracking data of the asteroid (101955) Bennu to constrain possible cosmic neutrino overdensities and Dark Matter in arXiv:2210.03749. (As always, the newsletter web version has direct links.)
