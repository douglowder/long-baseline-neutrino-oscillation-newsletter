Long-Baseline news, February 2018

*** Muon Ionization Cooling Experiment (MICE) progress

The MICE experiment concluded its successful data-taking run at the U.K.'s Rutherford Appleton Lab on Dec. 20, 2017. MICE efforts are now focused on delivering analysis results, with the first report of muon ionization cooling expected at IPAC18 29 April - 4 May, 2018 in Vancouver. https://ipac18.org/

*** NOvA progress

It was noticed that the new NOvA results presented last month doubled the data with 50% more beam. NOvA is now running with 650-710 kW. In 2018, 2.13e20pot has been delivered, with 2.09e20 pot recorded, or 98%. A recent maintenance campaign raised the FEB (front end board) fraction from 99.8% active to 99.97%. When g-2 is ready for physics (not yet), the NuMI cycle time will go from 1.33 s to 1.4 s, but FNAL is working to increase protons per pulse to compensate.

*** KATRIN progress

As reported in arXiv:1802.04167 , KATRIN uses a strong gaseous molecular tritium source and a large electrostatic spectrometer of MAC-E filter type, is used by the KATRIN experiment with the goal of improving the present sensitivity on the effective nu_e mass to 0.2 eV(90% CL). In 2016 they utilized 100 eV photoelectrons to cover the full distance on to the main detector. In July 2017 they used monoenergetic conversion electrons from the decay of 83Kr. KATRIN is currently preparing for the start of a five-year measurement phase.

*** NuWRO progress to Version 18.02

According to Volume 18 #2 (get it?) of NuWRO news, Version 18.02 is available from NuWRO. http://borg.ift.uni.wroc.pl/nuwro/ There are new NN cross sections and reweighting tools. https://nuwro.github.io/user-guide/

*** Progress on Fermilab Accelerator Improvement Projects

An upgrade for NOvA to 900 kW is planned formerly known as PIP-1+. It will increase proton intensity and address aging accelerator infrastructure. The 7 y plan includes making the target robust at 1 MW by FY19. The initial FY18 funding from DOE doesn't include AIPs. Shielding assessments for the Main Injector and target are in progress & expect approval this spring for higher ppp.

*** NOvA and T2K joint fits?

After a workshop to discuss joints fits, a NOvA & T2K statement: "The NOvA and T2K Collaborations are working towards the formation of a joint working group to enhance the measurements of neutrino oscillation parameters made by each Collaboration individually. The projected timescale of the NOvA-T2K working group is for production of a full joint neutrino oscillation analysis by 2021."

*** Double/Chooz/RENO joint fits?

There were workshops among the reactor neutrino collaborations in Seoul in 2016 and Paris in 2017. There might be one in 2018 in Hong Kong. The goal is to understand each others' analyses first and the final goal is to combine results.
