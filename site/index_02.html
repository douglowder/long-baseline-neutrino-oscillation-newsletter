SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS Two subscription methods are available. To receive the (~100 line) newsletter each month, the listname is "longbnews". You can subscribe by sending a message "subscribe longbnews firstname lastname" to [email protected] . Put your name, not your email, and put it in the body of the message, not the subject.
To get a one line message that the newsletter is available on the web, the listname is "longbnews_web". You can subscribe by sending a message "subscribe longbnews_web firstname lastname" to [email protected] . Instructions for additions/deletions/changes can be found at http://listserv.fnal.gov/users.html. For either list, you could also just contact: Maury Goodman. at [email protected] and send a message in any format that you like.
