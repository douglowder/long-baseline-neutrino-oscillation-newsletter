Long-Baseline news, June 2017

*** Single Phase ProtoDUNE Technical Design Report

ProtoDUNE-SP is the single-phase DUNE Far Detector prototype that is under construction and will be operated at the CERN Neutrino Platform in 2018. It is a crucial part of the DUNE effort towards construction of the first DUNE 10-kt fiducial mass module (17kt total) and is a significant experiment in its own right. With a total mass of .77kt, it represents the largest monolithic single- phase LArTPC detector to be built to date. The TDR describes in detail in its 165 pages the detector components, installation, software/computing and test beam specs. See arXiv:1706.07081

*** MINOS decommissioned

When MINOS was constructed in the Soudan Lab in the early days of the 21st century, there was an agreement with the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources to remove the detector when the experiment was complete. That "deconstruction" was started last fall and completed in the spring of 2017. http://www.virginiamn.com/mine/a-pretty-cool-experience/article_5175b704-9b31-11e6-a288-33acbf4bbfdc.html Some videos of the decommissioning can be found at http://www.soudan.umn.edu/decom/. A new tenant for the underground space hasn't yet been found. http://www.kbjr6.com/story/34146577/as-soudan-underground-lab-moves-experiments-search-continues-for-new-tenants

*** NuSTEC white paper studies neutrino interaction needs

Precision nu experiments rely on their interactions with bound nucleons in nuclei, so there is required a commensurate effort in the understanding and modeling of the hadronic and nuclear physics of these interactions, which is incorporated as a nuclear model in nu event generators. In a white paper prepared by the NuSTEC collaboration, they discuss in detail the impact of these interactions, especially the nuclear effects, on the measurement of neutrino properties using oscillation parameters as a central example. See arXiv:1706.03621 . Want to learn more? The NuSTEC school is 7-15 Nov at FNAL http://nustec.fnal.gov/school2017/.

*** NOvA searches for active-sterile neutrino mixing

The number of neutral current (NC) events doesn't change in the presence of active-active neutrino mixing, but decreases if active-sterile mixing takes place. NOvA sees 95 NC events, with 83.5 +-9.7(stat) +-9.4(syst) expected in the three-nu paradigm. In a 3+1 model, this limits theta24 < 20.8 degrees and theta34 < 31.2 degrees at 90%CL for delta msquared_41 between .05 eV^2 and 0.5 eV^2. See arXiv:1706.04592 .
