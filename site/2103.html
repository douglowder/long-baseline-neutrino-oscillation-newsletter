Long-Baseline news, March 2021

*** ICARUS (at Fermilab) sees first neutrinos

The first real ICARUS nu events from the Booster proton extraction inside a 100 microsecond window were recorded, centered around the beam spill gate. The PMT light signal is defined by at least 10 of the 180 PMT’s of each module with at least 50 phe each, which corresponds to an energy loss of about 3 MeV/PMT. At the nominal drift field of 500 V/cm, the longest time for collected e's is about 1 ms. The anode to cathode drift distance is 1.5 m.

*** Three leading technologies for neutrinoless double beta decay

DOE-NP has identified 3 leading technologies for a ton-scale 0nu2beta detector: 1. scintillating bolomotry, 2. enriched Ge, & 3. liquid xenon TPC. 1. CUORE has operated since March 2019 at 11 mK, which indicates readiness for a ton-scale bolometer. CUPID is making a technical design. 2. LEGEND is a combination of experimenters from GERDA and Majorana. One plan is for five 200-kg groupings of 76Ge. 3. nEXO is a design for a liquid Xenon cryostat submerged in an active water shield. All 3 plan have exceptionally challenging background constrains, < 1 count/ton-y. The DOE has monthly technical updates of LEGEND-100, nEXO & CUPID.

*** Two upcoming meetings for neutrinoless double beta decay

A "Portfolio Review" (aka downselect panel) will be 13-16 Jul 2021 to inform the U.S. investment strategy for 0nu2beta experiments. Then there will be a North American–European Summit 27-29 Sep. to see if common ground exists for an international approach to DBD investment. A reasonable goal is that there be two complementary ton-scale experiments worldwide to find DBD.

*** Three leading sites for neutrinoless double beta decay

Possible locations for a ton-scale 0nu2beta project are the Sanford Underground Research Facility in South Dakota, SNOlab in Sudbury, Canada, and the Gran Sasso Lab in Italy.

*** DUNE near detector (ND) Conceptual Design Report (CDR)

http://arxiv.org/abs/2103.13910. The DUNE ND CDR was approved by the Long-baseline Neutrino Committee and is available. Components include a liquid Ar TPC (ND-LAr), a magnetized argon target system (ND-GAr), A Precision Reaction-Independent Spectrum Measurement near detector (DUNE-PRISM) and a System for on-Axis Neutrino Detection (SAND). Other sections cover neutrino cross-section, BSM physics, SM physics, Facilities and Computing teams.

*** Define a neutrino

I tell people neutrinos are fundamental particles not in atoms. At a garage sale, I saw a set of World Book Encyclopedias with a dictionary, so I looked up "neutrino". It said, "A stable atomic particle having no electric charge and a mass too close to zero to be measured." I looked up "atomic" and it was "having to do with atoms". Dictionary.com & Merriam-Webster.com do better.
