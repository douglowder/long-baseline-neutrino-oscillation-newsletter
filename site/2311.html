Long-Baseline news, November 2023

*** Benchmarking report presented to HEPAP with a DUNE focus

A new report from the High Energy Physics Advisory Panel (HEPAP) stresses the importance of international collaboration in particle physics & argues that the U.S. should prioritize being a “partner of choice” for others. It finds that the U.S. is not always viewed as a reliable partner, largely due to unpredictable budgets and inadequate communication. LBNF/DUNE is the first U.S.-hosted international particle physics mega-project. It was launched as an international project with broad international participation. The treatment of LBNF/DUNE as a single DOE project (i.e., coupling the facility & experiment) made realizing DUNE as a full international partnership challenging. See the presentation and the report at: https://science.osti.gov/-/media/hep/hepap/pdf/202308/HEPAPBenchmarking_HEPAP_202308.pdf , https://science.osti.gov/-/media/hep/hepap/pdf/202311/HEPAPBenchmarking_Nov_2023.pdf

*** DUNE's Liquid Argon Near Detector Prototype is called 2x2

https://news.fnal.gov/2023/11/prototype-for-dune-detector-will-test-new-technology-that-can-handle-more-neutrinos/ The design for a Liquid Argon component of DUNE's near detector is 35 modules. Four slightly smaller modules, arranged in a square, make up a prototype to be installed on the main axis of the NuMI beam. The 2x2 will be located between two repurposed detectors from the MINERvA detector.

*** Atmospheric Neutrinos in Super-K I-V with neutron tagging

In arXiv:2311.05105 , Super-K reports on 6511 days of atmospheric neutrino events corresponding to 484.2 kiloton-years. This includes events from an expanded fiducial volume. It favors the normal mass ordering at the 92.3% confidence level. Fits give dmsq_32 = 2.40+0.07−0.09 e-3 eV^2 (ignoring a 1000 typo), sin^2(q_23) = 0.45 + 0.06 −0.03, and delta_CP = −1.75 +0.76 −1.25 This work is accompanied by a data release.

*** Improved limit from GERDA on neutrinoless 2e capture of 36Ar

In arXiv:2311.02214, the GERDA collaboration sees no 429.88 keV photon signal in its Germanium, and sets a 90% CL limit on two electron capture with no neutrinos on 36Ar of > 1.5 e22 y. The live time from GERDA phase I plus GERDA phase II is 4.34 y.
